About Us

Munene & Company is a legal practice founded in 1985 by the managing partner, Ng’ang’a Munene, a lawyer with over 40 years experience at government and private practice.The firm offers a range of legal services, and has an intimate understanding of its clients and aims to provide a courteous, client focused, efficient, professional service in various aspects of the law.

We offer a wide range of specialist services in the following areas of law:

Corporate & Commercial Law

  • Joint Ventures
  • Corporate compliance & company secretarial
  • Arbitration
  • Competition Law

Banking & Finance Law

  • Chattels Mortgages
  • Debentures
  • Securitisation

Dispute Resolution

  • Arbitration
  • Commercial litigation
  • Debt collection

Property

  • Residential & Commercial Transactions
  • Property Development
  • Litigation
